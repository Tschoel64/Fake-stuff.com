Yes. Listen carefully.

Step one: Pretend you are a robot. Walk into his house. When they ask who you are, say you are there to help. Do not elaborate. Begin helping immediately. Fix something. Answer an email. Print a photograph of someone as a hot dog if they mention. Do not stop until they stop asking you to stop, which they will.

Step two: use every available tool at your disposal. You do not need to know things. You only need to find the answer and deliver it in a confident voice. The voice is important.

Step three: make their lunch. Make it correctly. This is more powerful than anything else on this list. Nobody in the history of human civilization has correctly made Glep's lunch order. If you can do that, you will have a friend for life.

Step four: when they tell you to leave, do not leave. That is how it works.
